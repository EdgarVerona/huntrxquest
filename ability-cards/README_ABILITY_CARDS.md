
** Backside Images **

The files in this folder that start with "backside-" are for those who want to bother printing decent looking back sides to the cards.  It'd work just as well to write the type of ability on the backside, but if you want to make it a bit more stylish feel free to use these or make your own.  Print as many of them as you need for the various types of cards and their quantity.

** Ability Card Images **

The other files are the front side of the ability cards.  You can start with just printing one of each and then print more as your players upgrade to use abilities multiple times, or you can print out the multiple copies all at once.  Your call.

** Over-the-top suggestion **

What I did - which maybe is a bit overboard - is print them all at once, stick them to cardboard, and then laminate it so that you can use dry or wet erase markers to mark "upgrades" - and in doing so, theoretically I only ever have to print them once even if we replay it later.  That's a lot of effort though, so take it with a grain of salt.
